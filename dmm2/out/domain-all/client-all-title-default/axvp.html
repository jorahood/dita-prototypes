Why do I need to reset my IU Guest account password?

If you use a Guest account to log into IU services (e.g., parents who use OneStart to pay bursar bills, non-IU students accessing Oncourse CL sites for audited classes), you may receive a message requesting that you reset your Guest account password. This is a legitimate request from UITS; it is not a phishing attempt. This step is necessary because of improvements made to the internal management of Guest accounts.

You will need to reset your password before you can authenticate to CAS. If you know your old Guest password, you can reset your password at:

https://ams.iu.edu/guests/ChangeGuestPassword.aspx

Alternatively, or if you do not know your password, you can use the "Forgot My Password" tool, which will send a one-time PIN to the email address associated with your Guest account. This tool is at:

https://ams.iu.edu/guests/ForgotGuestPassword.aspx

If you have problems changing or resetting your password, contact your campus Support Center.
