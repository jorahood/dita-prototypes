Residential Technology Centers (RTCs)
RTCs will be accessible in residences that remain open during winter break; however, staffing for all IUB RTCs will end Thursday, December 17, at 4pm and resume Sunday, January 10, at 6pm.
Note: SC staff: Open residences are Campus View, Evermann, Tulip Tree, and University West. TCC will replenish the open sites with paper and toner during the winter break. If there is an IUB site problem, the resident should call the TCC office at 812-855-3802.
Student Technology Centers (STCs)
  • STCs located in IU Libraries will observe library hours.
  • The Information Commons and Music Library will observe this schedule (from the drop-down menu at the bottom, select the week you are interested in).
  • Unstaffed STCs will close on Saturday, December 19, and reopen on Sunday, January 10.
  • All STCs will be closed on December 24, 25, and 31, and January 1. Normal operating hours will resume on Sunday, January 10. For more, see IUB STCs.
